Help Ukraine in the West

Life Saving Medical Supplies

Non-profit aid to our Ukrainian Brethren!

Ukraine Aid in the West is a collaborative project between Trygg i Beredskap and a number of companies and private individuals in Western Sweden. In this way, we have a non-profit association behind us, while the focus is on gathering committed people through the Facebook group. Here we support each other through advice, tips, planning and implementation. We put aside political affiliations and rivalries and work towards a common goal.

  • Save Ukrainian Lives

    A Tourniquets mean life or death on the battlfield. Only the highest quality Cat 7 Medical grade work. We scour Sweden for supplies and drive them to Ukraine

  • Donate Trucks

    Through your genorousity we can often can not only Stuff the Truck full of medical supplies but we leave the vehicle behind. Trucks to help serve as battlefield ambulances

  • Critical Supplies

    As we deliver medical supplies we also learn what gear they need. This can include spare parts to a truck, motherboards, or electrical cabling. Your donation helps a frontline unit.
